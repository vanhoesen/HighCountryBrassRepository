Membership

All levels of talent are welcome to participate. Although composed primarily of former junior drum corps age-outs, HCB encourages younger members. It is not our intention to pull members from other local drum corps. In fact, we encourage members to participate in these groups. We are here just to offer an alternative. Members should be committed, flexible, patient and, above all else, positive in approach to the group. Equipment is available for members to use at rehearsals. A $50 deposit (applicable toward your dues) is required in order to take any equipment home.

Dues

As a non-profit performing group, paying dues in a timely manner is essential to ensure that funds are available to the corps for operation of the corps, uniforms, equipment and its trasportation, show design, music arrangement, instructional staff, association fees, and upcoming events. Dues for the 2013 season are $450 and travel expenses, including planned trips to competitions in Minnesota and to the 2013 DCA World Championships in Annapolis, MD are expected to be an additional $1,000. Dues MUST be paid in full to secure your spot within the corps. A payment plan is available to members who are unable to satisfy this expectation immediately.

Members now have more options for paying dues!

Pay Membership Dues In Person

Cash, checks, and credit cards will be accepted at rehearsals. You will receive a receipt for your records. Checks should be made out to High Country Brass.

Pay Membership Dues Online

High Country Brass dues can now be paid with a credit card online (via PayPal) in a single payment or in multiple payments over time. Your choice!
